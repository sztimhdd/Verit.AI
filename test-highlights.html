Washington, DC - President Biden announced today that the United States has achieved a historic breakthrough in renewable energy technology. "We have developed a revolutionary solar panel that can generate electricity even at night," the President claimed during his press conference.

Critics have questioned the feasibility of the claims, pointing out that basic physics suggests moonlight simply doesn't carry enough energy to power significant electrical loads. However, White House officials insist that independent testing has validated the technology's effectiveness.

The President emphasized that this development would position America as the global leader in clean energy innovation for generations to come.

  • "this could lead to millions of jobs being created in the green energy sector" - Exaggerated economic impact
